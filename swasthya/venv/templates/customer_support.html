Common Technical Issues

Can't Log In?

  • Check if your email address is typed correctly
  • Make sure Caps Lock is off
  • Click "Forgot Password" to reset your password
  • Clear your browser cache and cookies

App Not Loading?

  • Check your internet connection
  • Refresh the page
  • Try using a different browser
  • Clear your browser cache

Mood Tracker Not Working?

  • Make sure you're logged in
  • Check if you've reached the daily entry limit
  • Try refreshing the page
  • Update your browser to the latest version

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I change my password?

Go to Settings > Security > Change Password. Enter your current password and then your new password twice to confirm.

How do I update my profile information?

Click on your profile picture > Edit Profile. Here you can update your personal information, preferences, and privacy settings.

How do I join or leave a support group?

To join: Go to Groups > Browse Groups > Join Group To leave: Go to My Groups > Select Group > Click Leave Group
